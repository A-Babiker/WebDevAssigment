  • Uzungol

AWith it's name meaning Long Lake, Uzungol is a small village that has a (you guessed it) long lake. It is a popular tourist attraction and for a good reason. It is gorgeous, and has a decent amount of places to visit.

Seragulu is another small lake that is found in Uzungol, you can find lots of ducks and other birds swimming around, making it a fun lake to just chill at.

While not having much, Uzungol is still an amazing sight to witness, so you might want to schedule a couple days to stay at that majestic place and take a look around.

Besides the lakes, Uzungol Mosque is another one of the main attractions you can find. It is a quite nice beautiful building, and has religious importance.
