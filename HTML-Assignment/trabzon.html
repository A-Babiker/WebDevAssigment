A province by the coast, Trabzon is a place for those who love nature. This magical city is beautiful in all seasons and great locations, and a greater community.

Zigana is an elevated area full of hills. They are best enjoyed at winter, since that is when the beauty of snow comes in.

Ataturk Pavilion, or Ataturk House, is an important building that belonged to Ataturk. He led the Turkish people to their freedom, and his house is now open as a museum in his honor. Taking photos of the interior is not allowed, so you better come see it for yourself.

Trabzon is on the coast of the Black Sea. Due to it being a coastal area, it specializes in fishing. However, you could find hundreds of fields dedicated to growing hazelnut or tea leaves, both of which are widely consumed there.
